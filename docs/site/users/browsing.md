# Browsing your library

## Recently added

The home screen shows the 10 most recently added titles across your library. Tap any title to open it in your media player.

Mortar doesn't play media directly — tapping a title hands off to the player your operator has configured (typically Jellyfin).

## Continue watching

If your operator has linked your Mortar account to your media player account, the home screen shows a **Continue watching** row with titles you've partially watched or listened to.

If this row isn't visible, your accounts aren't linked yet — ask your operator to set it up.

## Can't find something?

If a title isn't showing in recently added, use [Search](./requesting) to check whether it's in the library. The **Available** badge means it's there and ready.
